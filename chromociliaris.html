Chromociliaris
Chromociliaris
En / 中

Chromociliaris is a new kind of digital creature uncovered after the leak. Similar to Ommatoporifera, they appear to be in clusters. A cluster of Chromociliaris typically consists of 80 to 400 individual entities. Within a cluster, they float around the space together in a collective manner. There are two repetitive phases of behaviours of a cluster. In one phase, all the individual entities group loosely together. During this phase, Chromociliaris entities act on their own most of the time, but some occasionally coordinated moves are found as well. After a while, they gather closer to each other and gradually become paler and paler. That's the other phase. During this phase, they look very similar to the creatures we observed before the leak. Some researchers see this as evidence of their existence before the leak. While they are switching between different phases their way of laying out within the cluster seems to be also switching between symmetric and asymmetric.

Chromociliaris是洩露事件後新發現的一種數字生物。與Ommtoporifera相類似的是，Chromociliaris總以集群的形式出現。通常來說一個Chromociliaris集群中有80到400個個體。他們以集體的方式移動。Chromociliaris集群的行為規律，由兩個不斷循環的階段組成。在其中一個階段，所有的個體鬆散地集合在一起。在此階段中，個體通常擁有自己的行為方式，但是他們亦不時有協調的動作。一段時間之後，他們聚攏得更加緊密，並且逐漸白化。這是第二個階段。在這個階段中他們看起來很像洩露事件之前我們看到的數字生物。有些研究者認為這是他們在洩露事件之前就存在的證據。當他們切換不同的階段時，個體在集群中的排布方式亦在對稱與非對稱間轉換。

Just like other digital creatures, our current knowledge of Chromociliaris is very limited, and there are still lots of mysteries lying around those little things. The most confusing one is whether it is a new species that evolved after the leak, as Chromociliaris appears first after the leak, but it is also the only creature that shows the monochromatic pre-leak feature after the event.

如同其他數字生物一樣，我們對Chromociliaris所知極少。這些小生命身上還有很多的謎團有待解開。其中最為令人疑惑的便是到底他們是否在洩露事件之前便已存在。Chromociliaris雖然在洩露事件之後才被發現，但是卻也是唯一保有洩露事件前黑白特質的數字生命。
